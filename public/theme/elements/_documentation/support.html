How to get support?

You have various ways to get support:

  • Send us an email to support@themes-lab.com
  • Send us a message via our Themeforest page: http://themeforest.net/user/themes-lab
  • Leave a comment to theme comments page.

Included Support

  • Fixing all issues related to original theme.
  • Responding to question regarding theme.
  • Providing regular updates depending on your needs and feedbacks.

Not Included Support

  • Customization of theme.
  • Specific support for third party plugins integrated in theme.

Need some new pages or advanced customization?

We are available for freelance work if you want customize theme or create new pages and elements. For more information, please send a direct email to support@themes-lab.com. Thanks in advance.

Important: In order to provide you fast and efficient support, please send detailed description of the issue, if needed with screenshot.
