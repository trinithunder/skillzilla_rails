Clients carousel

Theme use Owl Carousel to display clients carousel. For more information about Owl Carousel settings, go to sliders documentation.

Here you just have to put your clients logos inside owl item:

<div class="owl-carousel owl-theme clients m-b-100" data-items-desktop="4" data-items-tablet="3" data-items-mobile="2" data-plugin-options='{"autoplay":true,"autoplayTimeout":3500,"smartSpeed":1000,"items":4,"margin":0}'>
  <div class="item">
    <img src="logo-1.png" alt="" class="img-fluid">
  </div>
  <div class="item">
    <img src="logo-2.png" alt="" class="img-fluid">
  </div>
  <div class="item">
    <img src="logo-3.png" alt="" class="img-fluid">
  </div>
  <div class="item">
    <img src="logo-4.png" alt="" class="img-fluid">
  </div>
  <div class="item">
    <img src="logo-5.png" alt="" class="img-fluid">
  </div>
  <div class="item">
    <img src="logo-6.png" alt="" class="img-fluid">
  </div>
</div>

Of course, you can have less or more logos items. Here, 4 logos will be visible in large screen (desktop). Just change data-items-desktop value to change number of logos visible.
