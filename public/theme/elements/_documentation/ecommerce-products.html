Products list

Products list can be fullwidth or boxed, with various columns (2, 3, 4, 5 or 6). You can add a sidebar with multiples ecommerce widgets too.

Pagination markup

Here is the basic markup of list of a product inside a products list:

<nav class="pagination-wrapper">
  <ul class="pagination">
    <li class="active"><a href="#">1</a></li>
    <li><a href="#">2</a></li>
    <li><a href="#">3</a></li>
    <li><a href="#">4</a></li>
    <li><a href="#">5</a></li>
    <li>
      <a href="#" aria-label="Next">
      <span aria-hidden="true"><i class="nc-icon-outline arrows-1_tail-right"></i></span>
      </a>
    </li>
  </ul>
</nav>

Products with left sidebar

Here is the basic markup of list of products with left sidebar:

<div id="shop" class="shop-list">
  <!-- BEGIN TOP SORTING OPTIONS -->
  <div class="sort-options">
    <!-- TOP SORTING MARKUP -->
  </div>
  <!-- END TOP SORTING OPTIONS -->
  <div class="row">
    <!-- BEGIN PRODUCTS LIST -->
    <div class="products-list col-hg-10 col-lg-9 col-md-8 col-hg-push-2 col-lg-push-3 col-md-push-4">
      <div class="row">
        <div class="col-hg-3 col-lg-4 col-sm-6">
          <!-- PRODUCT 1 -->
        </div>
        <div class="col-hg-3 col-lg-4 col-sm-6">
          <!-- PRODUCT 2 -->
        </div>
      </div>
      <!-- PAGINATION MARKUP -->
    </div>
    <!-- END PRODUCTS LIST -->  

    <!-- BEGIN PRODUCTS SIDEBAR -->
    <aside class="products-sidebar col-hg-2 col-lg-3 col-md-4 col-hg-pull-10 col-lg-pull-9 col-md-pull-8">
      <div class="widget">
        <h3 class="widget-title">Widget title</h3>
        <!-- WIDGET CONTENT -->
      </div>
      <!-- OTHER WIDGETS CONTENT -->
    </aside>
    <!-- BEGIN END SIDEBAR -->      
  </div>
</div>
