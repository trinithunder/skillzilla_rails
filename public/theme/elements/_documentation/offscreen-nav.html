Offscreen Navigation

When your menu is ready, you can choose between large choices of header variants.

Navigation Position

Navigation can be at the top of your page, on the left and right side, or at the bottom on page load.

Offscreen navigation

Navigation can always be visible, or visible only when user click on a button (offscreen navigation). The button that toggle offscreen navigation is inside main-navigation id, in header, and have to toggle-menu class: There are various offscreen navigation available:

Left offscreen navigation.

Live example

Edit the toggle menu button like this:

<button class="toggle-menu" data-toggle="aside-menu" data-effect="push" data-position="left">
  <i class="nc-icon-outline ui-2_menu-dots"></i>
</button>
If you prefer that menu hover page content, change data-effect to hover: data-effect="hover". Here is the basic markup: 
<!-- BEGIN OFF ASIDE MENU -->
<aside id="off-aside-menu">
  <div class="aside-content">
    <div class="aside-inner">
      <div id="logo">
        <a href="#">
          <img src="assets/img/logo/your-logo.png" alt="logo">
        </a>
      </div>
      <nav id="main-menu">
        <!-- YOUR MENU -->
      </nav>
      <footer>
        <!-- NAVIGATION FOOTER -->
      </footer>
    </div>
  </div>
</aside>
<!-- END OFF ASIDE MENU -->
