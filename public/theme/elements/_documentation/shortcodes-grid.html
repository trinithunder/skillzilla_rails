Grid system

Live example

This template use the default bootstrap columns markup. You can read more about it here: http://getbootstrap.com/css/#grid.

Basically, it's a 12 grid system. This mean that page is divided into 12 columns. So, you can have 1 to 12 column. Here are some examples

1 column

<div class="row">
  <div class="col-md-12">
    <!-- YOUR CONTENT -->
  </div>
</div>

2 columns for device > 1200, 1 column for smaller device

<div class="row">
  <div class="col-lg-6">
    <!-- YOUR CONTENT -->
  </div>
  <div class="col-lg-6">
    <!-- YOUR CONTENT -->
  </div>
</div>

3 columns for device > 992, 1 column for smaller device

<div class="row">
  <div class="col-md-4">
    <!-- YOUR CONTENT -->
  </div>
  <div class="col-md-2">
    <!-- YOUR CONTENT -->
  </div>
  <div class="col-md-6">
    <!-- YOUR CONTENT -->
  </div>
</div>
