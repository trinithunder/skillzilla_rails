Accordion

Live example

Accordion, like tabs, allow to display grouped information and show only necessary content. It could be usefull for FAQ or services section for example.

Basic markup

<div class="panel-group panel-accordion" id="my-accordion">
  <div class="panel panel-default">
    <div class="panel-heading">
      <h4>
        <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#my-accordion" href="#collapseOne">
          Section 1 title
        </a>
      </h4>
    </div>
    <div id="collapseOne" class="panel-collapse collapse in">
      <div class="panel-body">
        Section 1 content
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="panel panel-default">
    <div class="panel-heading">
      <h4>
        <a class="collapsed" data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#my-accordion" href="#collapseTwo">
        Section 2 title
        </a>
      </h4>
    </div>
    <div id="collapseTwo" class="panel-collapse collapse">
      <div class="panel-body">
        Section 2 content
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="panel panel-default">
    <div class="panel-heading">
      <h4>
        <a class="collapsed" data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#my-accordion" href="#collapseThree">
          Section 3 title
        </a>
      </h4>
    </div>
    <div id="collapseThree" class="panel-collapse collapse">
      <div class="panel-body">
        Section 3 content
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

For dark version, add dark-accordion class to panel-accordion element.
