You can place reviewer image to the top or bottom, as well as quote icon.

For dark version, just add section-dark class to your parent section.

Bubble testimonials

Bubble testimonials can be left, center or right align.

Left align markup (default style): 
<div class="testimonial">
  <div class="testimonial-content">Review content.</div>
  <div class="testimonial-meta">
    <img src="review-img.jpg" alt="">
    <div class="testimonial-info">
      <p class="testimonial-name">
        Reviewer name
      </p>
      <p class="testimonial-subtitle">
        Additional info
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
Center align markup: 
<div class="testimonial testimonial-center">
  <div class="testimonial-content">Review content.</div>
  <div class="testimonial-meta">
    <img src="review-img.jpg" alt="">
    <div class="testimonial-info">

        Reviewer name
      </p>

        Additional info
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
