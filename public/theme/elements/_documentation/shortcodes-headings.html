Headings

Carefully craft typography to create beautiful design.

Live example


Basic markup

To add some options to your titles, like subtitle and separator, wrap your title into a title class. Here is the basic markup

<div class="title">
  <h1>Your title</h1>
  <p>Optional subtitle goes here</p>
</div>

Customize alignment

Title can be left, center or right align.

To center your title, add title-center class to your title:

<div class="title title-center">
  <h1>Your title</h1>
  <p>Optional subtitle goes here</p>
</div>

To align your title on right side, add title-right class to your title:

<div class="title title-right">
  <h1>Your title</h1>
  <p>Optional subtitle goes here</p>
</div>

Dashed line bottom

To add a small dashed line below your title, add title-line-bottom class to your title:

<div class="title title-center title-line-bottom">
  <h1>Your title</h1>
  <p>Optional subtitle goes here</p>
</div>

Normal line

To add a line at the same level than your title text, add title-line class to your title:

<div class="title title-line-bottom">
  <h1>Your title</h1>
  <p>Optional subtitle goes here</p>
</div>
